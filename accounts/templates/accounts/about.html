About CivicEye

Smart Civic Complaint Management System

CivicEye is a platform designed to bridge the gap between citizens and municipal authorities. It enables users to report civic issues, track complaints, and ensure accountability through a transparent system.

The system uses role-based access for Citizens, Workers, Surveyors, and Admins to efficiently manage complaints, assign tasks, and monitor progress.

Our goal is to create smarter cities by empowering citizens and improving government response systems.
